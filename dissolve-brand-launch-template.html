Month-by-Month Launch Plan

May 2026 → March 2027. Every task mapped to a month.
⚠️
Critical path items are marked with 🔴. Delaying these will push your launch date. All other items have some float.
May – June 2026
🏗 Foundation & Research
  • 🔴 Define your product line (which 3–5 SKUs to launch with)
  • 🔴 Research contract manufacturers (CM) — request NDAs + intro calls
  • Conduct competitor analysis: Blueland, HiBar, Ethique, Bite
  • Identify your target customer persona (eco-conscious? budget-first? luxury?)
  • Register your business entity (LLC) and EIN
  • Open a dedicated business bank account
  • Begin trademark name search (USPTO TESS)
  • Hire a cosmetic formulation consultant or connect with a CM R&D team
July – August 2026
🧪 Formulation & Brand Direction
  • 🔴 Sign contract manufacturer agreement — begin formulation development
  • 🔴 File trademark application with USPTO ($250–350/class)
  • Commission first round of formula samples
  • Begin brand naming workshop (shortlist 10 → 3 → 1)
  • Define brand values, sustainability story, and positioning statement
  • Choose aesthetic direction: premium minimalist, playful eco, clinical clean?
  • Draft ingredient lists and begin EU/FDA compliance review
  • Decide: dissolvable pods, tablets, or bars? (affects packaging and COGS)
September – October 2026
🎨 Brand Identity & Safety Testing
  • 🔴 Commission product safety assessments (PCPC, EU Cosmetics Regulation)
  • 🔴 Finalize brand name, logo, and visual identity system
  • Design packaging (primary + secondary): must be plastic-free or minimal
  • Build brand guidelines doc (colors, fonts, voice, do's/don'ts)
  • Photograph sample products for early content creation
  • Commission 3D renders of packaging for pre-launch marketing
  • Register for FDA cosmetic facility registration (required post-MoCRA 2023)
  • Begin eco-claim substantiation documentation (no greenwashing)
November – December 2026
🛒 Store Build & Pre-Launch Prep
  • 🔴 Build and soft-launch Shopify store (no traffic yet — internal review only)
  • 🔴 Finalize production run quantity and place manufacturing order
  • Set up Klaviyo email flows: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase
  • Set up Gorgias or Zendesk for customer support
  • Configure subscription app (Recharge or Skio)
  • Set up Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel
  • Build pre-launch waitlist landing page ("Be first to dissolve the norm")
  • Begin influencer outreach — identify 20 micro-creators (50k–300k) for seeding
  • Develop 10 SEO blog drafts (target long-tail: "best plastic-free shampoo")
  • Photograph final product for launch — lifestyle + product-on-white
January – February 2027
🚀 Pre-Launch & Inventory Arrival
  • 🔴 Receive and QC first production run inventory
  • 🔴 Ship inventory to 3PL warehouse (or set up self-fulfillment)
  • Seed product to influencers — embargo until launch week
  • Film hero "dissolve demo" video for TikTok and Reels
  • Write and schedule all launch-week emails (3–5 email sequence)
  • Build paid social creative: 5+ video ads, 3+ static ads
  • Set up Google Shopping feed and run test purchases
  • Brief customer support team on FAQs and returns policy
  • Do a full test order: place, fulfill, track, return
  • Set up affiliate/referral program (Impact or Refersion)
March 2027
🌟 Launch Month
  • 🔴 Open store to public — send waitlist launch email
  • 🔴 Go live on paid social (Meta + TikTok) Day 1
  • Influencer content goes live (coordinated embargo lift)
  • Publish first 5 SEO blog posts
  • Submit press release to sustainable beauty journalists
  • Monitor orders, fulfillment SLA, and CS tickets daily
  • Optimize paid ads weekly based on ROAS data
  • Collect first reviews — set up Okendo or Judge.me prompts
  • Launch referral program to early customers
  • Hold weekly team review: revenue, CAC, return rate, NPS

🌿 Naming territories to explore

Scientific/elemental, minimalist one-word, verb-based action names, nature-rooted, suffix plays (–ly, –co, –ery). Avoid generic eco-words: "green," "pure," "clean."

📸 Photography priorities

The "dissolve moment" is your hero shot. Film a tablet dropping into water in slow motion — this becomes your homepage, your TikTok, your ad creative. Plan this shoot carefully.

Phase 03

Legal & Compliance

Cosmetics are regulated differently in every market. Get legal review early — before you print packaging.
⚠️
MoCRA (2023) — The Modernization of Cosmetics Regulation Act now requires US cosmetic brands to register facilities and list products with the FDA. This is new as of 2023. Toothpaste with fluoride is classified as an OTC drug — different rules apply.
